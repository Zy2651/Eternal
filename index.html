Mission Statement

Our mission is to host the best events, manage the best clubs, and create the best community. In the future, we plan on expanding into esports and content creation.


Our Events

We partner with organizations and content creators alike to assemble the best events for the Brawl Stars community. In order to include everyone, our events are free to play for anyone to play. On top of having a diverse audience, we have world class competition providing viewers and participants with an unforgettable experience.

Our Clubs

The clubs we own are powered by our diligent and inteligent staff members and,most importantly, our members. We run clubs for anyone: ambitious grinders, newcomers, people who want a belonging, or people who want an active club to complete monthly and exclusive events. By providing clubs with unique purposes, our organization will continue to build quality clubs
